About This Website

This is a simple, text-based website built with pure HTML and CSS. It's designed to be fast, accessible, and easy to maintain.

Why Simple?

In a world of complex web applications and heavy frameworks, sometimes it's refreshing to go back to basics. This site proves that you can create something clean and professional without all the bells and whistles.

Technical Details

This website is:

Hosted on: GitHub Pages

Domain: Custom domain through Namecheap

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3

No JavaScript: Loads instantly, works everywhere

Mobile-friendly: Responsive design that works on all devices

About Me

I'm someone who appreciates the elegance of simplicity. Whether it's in web design, writing, or life in general, I believe that clarity and purpose matter more than complexity.

This website serves as both a personal space and a demonstration that effective web presence doesn't require cutting-edge technology - just good content and thoughtful design.
