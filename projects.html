My Work & Interests

Here are some projects and areas of interest that I'm passionate about:

This Website

A demonstration of simple, effective web design using only HTML and CSS. Hosted on GitHub Pages with a custom domain, proving that you don't need complex technology to create something useful and beautiful.

Minimalist Web Design

I'm interested in the philosophy of minimalism as applied to web design. How can we communicate effectively while removing unnecessary elements? This site is an experiment in that direction.

Static Site Advocacy

Static websites load faster, are more secure, and require less maintenance than dynamic sites. I believe they're perfect for many use cases that currently over-rely on complex solutions.

Digital Simplicity

In our hyper-connected world, I'm exploring how we can use technology intentionally rather than being overwhelmed by it. This includes everything from simple websites to mindful digital habits.

Future Projects

I'm always working on something new. Check back here periodically to see what I'm up to. Future projects might include more websites, writing, or experiments in simple technology solutions.

Philosophy

All of these projects share a common thread: the belief that simple, thoughtful solutions are often better than complex ones. Whether it's code, design, or life in general, I try to ask "what's the simplest way to solve this effectively?"
